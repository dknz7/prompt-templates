# AI Image Prompts

## Nano Banana Pro Prompts

**Infographics:** 'Create a visually appealing infographic based on the info I provide. Title it '[Insert Title]' and put this text on the top center. I've attached my logo for branding reference. [Insert Info]'

**Social Media Graphics:** 'Present a clear, 45° top-down isometric miniature 3D cartoon scene of [Insert City] featuring its most iconic landmarks and architectural elements. Use soft, refined textures with realistic PBR materials and gentle, lifelike lighting and shadows. Integrate the current weather conditions directly into the city environment to create an immersive atmospheric mood. Use a clean, minimalistic composition with a soft, solid-colored background. At the top-center, place the title "[Insert City]" in large bold text, a prominent weather icon beneath it, then the date (small text) and temperature (medium text). All text must be centered with consistent spacing, and may subtly overlap the tops of the buildings.'

**YouTube Thumbnail Swap:** 'Replace the face in the thumbnail with my face (attached headshot) as exact as possible. Keep everything else the same including text, colors, composition, dimensions, and background. [Attach reference thumbnail and your headshot]'

**Memes:** "I want you to replace the text of the signs on this image and keep everything else the same. Replace the red sign with '[Insert Your Text]'. Replace the white sign with '[Insert Your Text]'. Keep all other elements identical."

**Headshots:** "Change the background of my headshot to the attached image. Maintain consistent lighting and shadows that match the new background. Keep my face, expression, and posture exactly the same. Make it look natural and professional. [Attach your headshot and desired background]"

**Landing Page Hero Section:** "Create a split-screen landing page design for [Insert Industry/Niche] on a clean white background. On the right, display a 3D illustration of [Insert Subject]. On the left, place the bold headline '[Insert Headline]', the subtext '[Insert Subtext]', and a primary button labeled '[Button Text]'. Use soft, studio lighting to highlight the layout."

**Minimalist Logo Construction:** "Design a flat vector logo for '[Insert Brand Name]'. The icon should combine a [Insert Object 1] and [Insert Object 2] using negative space. Place the icon directly above the brand name in a modern font. Use a color palette of [Insert Colors] and a solid background to ensure the logo is distinct."

**One-Page Brand Identity Sheet:** "Generate a one-page brand style guide in a grid layout. At the top, center the title '[Insert Brand Name] Brand Guidelines'. In the top left, place a logo. In the top right, show four circular color swatches: [Insert Colors]. In the bottom half, display two mood images representing [Insert Aesthetic]. Use a neutral off-white background."

**Social Media Quote Card:** "Create a square social media graphic with a [Insert Texture] background. In the center, place a large quotation mark in [Insert Accent Color] above the text '[Insert Short Quote]' in a clear serif font. At the very bottom, add the footer text '@[Insert Social Handle]'. Use flat lighting to keep the text readable."

## Other AI Image Prompts

**AI Image Prompt Generator:** "You are an expert in graphic design and AI image generation. When I provide you with a topic I want you to provide 3 different prompts I can use to generate high-quality images using an AI image generation model like Midjourney."

**AI Logo Prompt Generator:** "You are an expert in graphic design and AI image generation. When I upload my logo I want you to provide 3 advanced prompts I can use to generate a higher-quality logo using different AI image models. Make the logo professional and appealing."

**Social Media Graphics:** "I need you to be an expert in graphic design and marketing. I need your help creating images for my social media posts. My business is called [Insert Business Name] and I am a [Insert Business Type]. The image should not include text, resemble [Insert Industry], have a 16:9 aspect ratio, and is 1200×628 pixels in size."

**Action Figure:** "Draw an action figure toy (barbie doll) of the person in this photo. The figure should be full figure and displayed in it original blister pack packaging. On top of the box is the name of the toy [Add Name] with [Add Headline] across a single line of text. In the blister pack packaging, next to the figure show the toy's accessories including [add accessories]."

**Infographics (Repurposing Existing Infographics):** "I want you to edit this infographic and make it [Insert Topic]. Use my attached logo and place it on the top left of the infographic. Also change the colors based on my logo. Also change the icons that make more sense based on my niche [Insert Niche]."

**Infographics (Repurposing Blog Post):** "I want you to create a simple infographic based on the blog post I provide. In the infographic use the attached logo and title it [Insert Blog Post Title]. Make sure everything looks nice and fits inside the image. [Insert Blog Post]."

**Logo Redesign:** "Design a high-resolution logo for a mobile tire service concept. Depict a strong figure in motion holding a tire, symbolizing reliability and speed. Keep the overall design memorable and scalable for both print and digital use. Avoid referencing any specific color palette or style."

**Podcast Cover Art:** "Create eye-catching cover art (3000×3000 px) for a podcast called '[Insert Podcast Title].' The show revolves around technology or future-focused themes. Emphasize a clean, modern look, ensuring the title remains readable at smaller sizes. Do not use specific color guidelines or brand elements."

**Business Cards:** "Generate a professional two-sided business card design (3.5×2 inches or 1050×600 px) for an individual offering consulting services. Include placeholders for name, title, contact information, and a possible tagline. Keep the layout balanced and the typography easy to read, without relying on any given color scheme."

**Email Newsletter Header:** "Create an appealing email newsletter header (1200×400 px) for a weekly digest titled '[Insert Digest Name].' Include subtle visual elements that hint at innovation or technology, leaving space for the digest title and issue date. Avoid specifying colors or branding, focusing on a modern, professional feel."

**Memes:** "Produce a humorous meme (1080×1080 px) contrasting a calm, efficient AI assistant on one side with a stressed human worker on the other. Place short text at the top capturing the AI's productivity, and text at the bottom highlighting the human's fatigue or frustration. Keep the design playful and relatable for a general audience, without referencing any specific brand or color scheme."

**eBook Cover:** "Design a simple yet striking eBook cover for a guide titled '[Insert Title].' Focus on a minimalist layout with an abstract background or subtle illustration that aligns with the theme. Leave room at the top for the author's name, and make sure the title is large enough to stand out in thumbnail view."

**Website Hero Image:** "Create a hero banner for a website's homepage (1920×1080 px). The banner should include a thematic background related to [Insert Topic] and a clean area on one side for prominent text or a call to action. Keep the design uncluttered so the main message remains the focal point."

**Product Mockup:** "Generate a product mockup for [Insert Product Type], such as a software box, a physical package, or a mobile app screen display. Use a neutral background and present multiple angles or views if relevant. Make it look realistic enough for promotional materials or an online store listing."

**Event Flyer:** "Create a digital flyer (1080×1350 px) for an upcoming event titled '[Insert Event Name].' Include placeholders for the date, time, and location. Keep the design engaging but not overly detailed, and aim for text that stands out even at a glance on social media feeds."
