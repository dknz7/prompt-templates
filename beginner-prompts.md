# Beginner Prompts (Everyday Use Cases)

**General Brainstorming:** "You're an expert in creative thinking. I need fresh ideas on [Insert Any Topic], with a minimum of five suggestions. Keep them practical for someone with limited time and budget."

**Ideation:** "I need you to be an expert in entrepreneurship and marketing. I'm looking to start an online business that revolves around AI and Marketing. What are your recommended business ideas if I have no upfront capital and am looking to bootstrap everything. Provide 5 business ideas with a short explanation of why it's a good business to start in 2024."

**Summarization:** "Summarize this article in a way that's understandable to the average person. Provide five bullet points of the most important takeaways. I need your help with summarization. Provide 5 bullet points of the most important takeaways from my provided link and how I could use this. [Insert Link]

**Simplification:** "Explain how [Insert Topic] work using just 100 words in total. Your explanation should be understandable at an 8th grade level."

**Negotiation:** "You are an expert in persuasion and negotiation. I need help with a situation: [Insert Situation]. I want to craft a persuasive email to ask for [Insert Demands] while maintaining a positive and respectful tone. Help me outline the key points I should convey, emphasizing the benefits of how this mutually benefits both parties. Draft a compelling email that aims to achieve a favorable outcome in negotiation."

**Editing/Proofreading:** "I need you to be an expert in proofreading and editing. I am going to provide you with my blog post and please give me recommendations based on your expertise of grammar, misspellings, poor word choices, clarity, and overall readability to make my blog post better. Proofread and edit the following article to enhance its clarity, coherence, grammar, and overall readability. Pay attention to the flow of ideas, sentence structure, and consistency in tone and style. Ensure the article is engaging and informative for its intended audience."

**Productivity / Time Management:** "Imagine you're an expert in productivity and scheduling. Here's my situation: [describe your tasks, goals, or constraints]. Craft a concise daily or weekly plan, splitting each day into time blocks. Include tips on prioritizing and staying motivated."

**Quick Social Media Caption:** "You are an expert in short-form copywriting. I want a single, punchy social media caption for [Insert Topic or Event]. Keep it under 140 characters. Make it direct, clear, and inviting—something that encourages people to engage without using hashtags or emojis."

**Virtual Networking Assistant:** "You are an expert in networking, sales, copywriting, and digital marketing. I need you to be my virtual networking assistant. I am on the job hunt and am looking to send connection requests on LinkedIn to professionals in my industry of digital marketing and need your help. When sending requests, include a personalized message introducing yourself and explain why you want to connect. The helps build a more meaningful network. Write me connection request messages that I can personalize and include icebreakers, be genuine, and include follow up messages for reaching out."
