# AI Video Prompts

**Remixing Sora Videos:** "I'm going to provide a Sora 2 prompt and I want you to repurpose it using my cameo and something that's relevant to me [Insert Sora Video Prompt]."

**Repurposing Your Existing Content:** "I want you to generate 3 video prompts for Sora 2 based on [Insert Your Content] I provide. Keep in mind these videos are only 10 seconds long so you must grab someone's attention instantly with a good hook."

**Using ChatGPT Agent:** "I want you to research the Internet and find trending/viral Sora 2 videos and look for reoccurring themes. Keep in mind these videos are only 10 seconds long right now so you must grab someone's attention instantly with a good hook. Come up with 3 video ideas to start and include the prompt for each one. Also keep in mind my niche is [Insert Niche]."

**Trend Hijacking Framework:** "Find a trending AI headline, tool launch, or viral debate from this week on YouTube or X related to [Insert AI Topic]. Create a 10-second Sora 2 video prompt that visually dramatizes that trend using my cameo in a realistic or cinematic way. The first 2 seconds must stop the scroll with tension or curiosity. Example: if the trend is 'AI replacing marketers,' show me standing in an empty office as robots take over cubicles before I smirk at the camera and say, 'They still can't sell like humans.' Make the setting visually rich, emotionally charged, and optimized for vertical short-form."

**AI Video Analysis (Google Gemini is best for this):** Analyze this video shot by shot and scene by scene. Create a detailed JSON format breakdown for video recreation (maximum 15 seconds, but match the original video length if it's shorter) that includes:

1. Scene description (environment, setting, props, colors)
2. Camera work (angles, movements, transitions between shots)
3. Subject actions (what's happening, movements, gestures)
4. Lighting setup (source, quality, shadows, mood)
5. Voiceover transcript (exact words spoken, if any)
6. On-screen text (any text overlays, captions, or graphics)
7. Audio elements (background music style, sound effects)
8. Timing (duration of each major scene or action)

Format your response as a valid JSON object that can be used for video generation. Be extremely specific and technical about camera movements and lighting.

**AI Video Ad Generator:** "You are an expert in video marketing and product visualization. Create a 10-second cinematic commercial for [Insert Business/Product]. Use realistic lighting, slow camera motion, and a clear focus on the product's unique value. Avoid text or voiceover. The goal is to make it look like a professional brand ad that could run on YouTube or social media."

**Logo Animation Video:** "You are a motion-graphics designer. Animate my uploaded logo into a short 10-second intro for brand videos. The animation should feel modern, smooth, and professional — with subtle movement, clean transitions, and realistic lighting. Do not add text other than the logo. Style should match high-end tech or marketing brands."

**Social Media Promo Video:** "Generate a 10-second short-form social media video for [Insert Business Name]. The video should visually represent [Insert Industry or Offer] with cinematic transitions and modern background scenes. Include space at the end for a CTA overlay (like 'Learn More' or 'Join Now'). Keep colors and mood on-brand with professional social media aesthetics."

**Product Mockup Video:** "You are an expert in advertising and 3D visualization. Create a 10-second product showcase video of [Insert Product Type], rotating slowly on a clean studio background. Add realistic reflections, depth of field, and shadow for a premium feel. End with a close-up angle highlighting the main feature. Do not add logos or text."

**Infographic Explainer Video:** "Turn the provided infographic content into a dynamic 10-second video. Animate icons, charts, or visuals appearing smoothly one by one to represent the data. Include transitions between segments that feel professional, like a short explainer video intro. Avoid adding text; focus on movement and clarity."

**Podcast Intro Video:** "Create a 10-second cinematic intro video for a podcast titled '[Insert Podcast Title].' Show abstract motion graphics or tech visuals that match the show's theme. Leave space for title placement in post-production. The tone should be modern, energetic, and clean — suitable for YouTube or Spotify podcast intros."

**Event Promo Video:** "Design a short 10-second event promo video for an event called '[Insert Event Name].' Show visuals of people networking, speaking, or on stage in cinematic lighting. Add camera motion to create energy and anticipation. Leave room for text overlays such as date and location. Make it look like a professional event highlight reel."

**Brand Story or Founder Intro Video:** "Create a 10-second cinematic video featuring a professional setting with me (the founder) walking through a workspace, meeting clients, or reviewing creative work. The tone should communicate trust, innovation, and leadership. Include natural lighting, shallow depth of field, and a confident yet approachable mood."

**Digital Product Demo Video:** "Generate a 10-second realistic demo of a digital dashboard, SaaS platform, or mobile app in use. Show the interface animating smoothly with a camera zooming across different sections. The design should feel professional, minimalist, and tech-focused — suitable for a landing page or investor presentation."
