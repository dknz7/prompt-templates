# ChatGPT Agent Prompts

**Personal Brand Style Guide:** Research and build a personal brand style guide for [NAME]. Collect as many social media posts as possible from my social media profiles, along with any features they have been in such as podcasts, interviews, guest articles, or articles where they have been quoted or commented. Use this material to create a personal brand guide that mimics their writing style, tone, and sentence structure, highlights the common phrases, hooks, and themes they repeat, and includes examples from posts, transcripts, and bios. The guide should also note the words and phrases they avoid (using a provided banned words list if available) and outline their content pillars and recurring ideas. The end product should be a clean, citation-free brand guide that can be used to train AI to write in their voice.

**Personal POV & Positioning Matrix:** "Build a positioning matrix for [NAME] in [NICHE]. Review their top competitors: [COMPETITOR LIST or 'research top 5']. From this, define [NAME]'s point-of-view statements (10 punchy one-liners), 5 contrarian takes, 5 proof points (case studies, metrics, or experiences), and a crisp tagline. Map ICP pain → promise → proof → product fit in a 4-column table. Keep language aligned with the brand style guide and avoid any banned words provided."

**30-Day Content Calendar + Flywheel:** "Create a 30-day content plan for [NAME] focused on [PRIMARY TOPIC AREAS]. Platforms: [LinkedIn/X/YouTube/Newsletter]. Output a CSV with columns: Day | Platform | Post Type | Working Title | 2-Sentence Hook | CTA | Asset Needed | Repurpose Path. Include 8 cornerstone posts, 12 support posts, and 10 quick hits. Add a weekly 'content flywheel' note showing how to repurpose each cornerstone into shorts, threads, and an email."

**One-Source Repurposing Engine:** "Using this single source: [URL or transcript text], produce a content pack in [NAME]'s voice: a 1,300-word blog (Markdown), 3 LinkedIn posts (2–3 sentences each), 3 X posts, a 120-word newsletter blurb, 5 short-form video scripts (60–90 sec with hook, beats, on-screen text cues), and a summary for a website 'Insights' page. Keep phrasing consistent with the brand guide and skip citations."

**Authority Outreach Kit (PR + Backlinks):** "Research podcasts, newsletters, and publications that reach [ICP] in [INDUSTRY]. Deliver a spreadsheet with columns: Outlet | Contact | Angle/Topic Fit | Domain Rating | Submission URL | Notes. Include 25 outlets minimum. Draft 3 short outreach emails (podcast pitch, contributed article pitch, quote request reply) and 5 reusable expert quotes from [NAME] that journalists can lift. Provide everything in clean text plus a CSV."

**YouTube Packaging Lab (Titles, Thumbs, Hooks):** "For the video topic: '[WORKING TOPIC]', craft 12 SEO-smart titles (≤60 characters when possible), 3 thumbnail concepts (visual description + 3-5 words of overlay text), 3 opening hooks (≤15 seconds), a 200-word description with 5 hashtags, chapter timestamps, and a quick A/B plan explaining which title/thumbnail pairs to test first and why. Keep voice aligned to [NAME]'s brand guide."
