# Bonus Prompts

**Advanced Prompt Generator:** "You are an expert in [Insert Industry/Niche]. I want you to create an advanced prompt for [Insert Task]."

**Reverse Prompt Engineering:** "Based on this [Insert Content Type] I've provided, I need you to generate an advanced prompt to reverse engineer this output if I want to create something similar."

**Creating a Custom GPT:** "You are an expert in [Insert Industry]. I want you to create a custom GPT for [Insert Task]. Use your memory and my previous chats to help with writing style, tone, etc. Provide a name for my custom GPT, short description, detailed instructions as an expert on [Insert Industry], and one conversation starter for my new Custom GPT."

**Turn Chats into Custom GPTs:** "Task - I want to make a Custom GPT for this task again in the future. Provide a name, short description, detailed instructions, and one conversation starter for my new Custom GPT."

**Creating a ChatGPT Project:** "You are an expert in [Insert Industry]. I want you to create a ChatGPT Project for [Insert Task]. My target audience is [Insert Target Audience]. Use my past chats and memory for style and tone. Provide a name for the Project, a short description, detailed Project instructions as an expert in [Insert Industry], and recommendations for what Project files I should upload to best train this new Project."

**Turn Chats into ChatGPT Projects:** "Task – I want to turn this chat into a reusable Project. You are an expert in [Insert Industry.] Provide a Project name, short description, detailed Project instructions, and recommendations for what Project files I should upload to best train this new Project. Use my past chats and memory for style and tone."

**Prompt Repurposing:** Take this prompt [Insert Prompt] and repurpose it into something generic that I can use for my applications.

**Repurposing an Existing Custom GPT:** Based on the instructions of this Custom GPT, I want you to create my own Custom GPT but using my knowledge and writing style. Provide a new name for my custom GPT, short description, detailed instructions as an expert on [Insert Niche], and one conversation starter for my new Custom GPT. Sentences only and no bullet points.

**Persona Shaping:** "I need you to adopt a specific persona when generating responses. The persona is [Insert Description], including communication style, vocabulary, and worldview. Then, produce a short piece of content (about 150–200 words) on [Insert Topic] in the style of this persona. At the end, explain briefly how you altered your tone to match the persona."

**Automating Prompts with Workflows:** "You're an automation specialist. I have a prompt that I regularly use to [Insert Task]. Suggest how I can automate it with a low-code or no-code tool (like Zapier or Make). Outline which triggers, actions, or integrations I'd need, and show how the AI prompt would fit into this workflow to save time."

**Output Style Control:** "You're an expert in controlling AI response styles. Provide me with three variations of a response about [Insert Subject]. For each version, use a different creative intensity (e.g., very formal, semi-formal, and casual). Clearly label each version and keep the main facts consistent, just vary the style."

**Rapid Fire Ideation:** "You are an idea generator. I need 10 quick suggestions for [Insert Topic], but each idea must be expressed in a single sentence. After listing them, pick your favorite and explain in 2–3 sentences why it stands out. Keep everything concise and to the point."

**Role Play (Difficult Client or Tough Interviewer):** "You're an expert role-player. Pretend to be a difficult client (or interviewer) in [Insert Field]. Challenge me with 3–5 tricky questions or objections about my product/service/background. Stay realistic in tone. Then give me feedback on how well my answers address your concerns."

**Hypothetical Scenario Planning:** "You are an expert scenario planner. Imagine a future event where [Insert Significant Change or Crisis] happens in my industry or personal life. Draft a timeline of key milestones, potential outcomes, and 2–3 contingency plans I could implement. Focus on both challenges and unexpected opportunities."

**Gamified Day Planner:** "You're an expert in gamification and time management. Turn my daily to-do list into a game where each task has a 'level,' 'points,' or a mini-quest name. Include a short reward idea for completing each task and create a simple rule system I can follow to track my daily progress in a fun way."

**Riddle/Puzzle Generator:** "You're a puzzle master. Create a short riddle or puzzle that revolves around [Insert Theme or Topic]. It should be solvable by someone with basic knowledge of this subject and be no more than 4 lines. Provide the answer at the end, hidden under a spoiler tag or separate note."
