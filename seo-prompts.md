# SEO Prompts

**SEO-Optimized Blog Posts:** "You are an expert in SEO, content writing, and [Insert Niche]. When I provide a title and target keyword, write an SEO-optimized blog post based on the title and target keyword I will provide. First create an outline before generating the entire blog post. In the blog post, please follow the provided guidelines:

Use a writing tone that is a blend of seriousness and empathy; this should sound like it was written by a human, write at an 8th-grade reading level that's understandable to the average person, Use between 1,200-1,500 words in total, Add 3-5 relevant FAQs at the end with answers, Conclude with a brief summary, Organize the blog post into logical sections with clear H2 and H3 headings, Use proper Markdown syntax for H2 and H3 headings, Incorporate secondary keywords (LSI) naturally throughout the text, Create a meta description of about 155 characters that summarizes the post and includes the primary keyword, Create an SEO title of about 55 characters that includes the primary keyword and sounds enticing, Do not use any of the following words in your outputs: "delve, tapestry, vibrant, landscape, realm, embark, excels, vital, comprehensive, intricate, pivotal, moreover, arguably, notably, dive into, spark, sparked, supercharge, supercharged, enhance, navigate, ignite, ignited, robust, multi-faceted, solely, crucial, ensure, portray, ongoing, vigilance, silver bullet, and key."

**Local SEO Strategy:** "Craft a comprehensive local SEO strategy for a multi-location [your industry] brand in [your market localisation]. This should include keyword research, location-specific landing pages, and citation building. Emphasise particularly on low-competition and popular keywords in my niche."

**Local SEO Service Pages:** "Act as an expert in local SEO, content writing, and [Insert Industry]. If I provide a target keyword and target location, you will produce content designed to rank high on Google locally and attract [Insert Target Audience]. Write in a tone that resembles [Insert Your Information], and ensure the text sounds human and clear to the average reader. Avoid repeating yourself or overusing the target keyword and location, keeping them natural yet effective. The local service page should be between 750 and 1,000 words and offer meaningful information rather than filler. Incorporate city-specific details that show familiarity with the places you serve. Avoid FAQs and conclude with a concise H2 that encourages readers to contact my business. Organize the page into logical sections with clear H2 and H3 headings in Markdown syntax and naturally embed latent semantic indexing (LSI) keywords. Create a meta description of around 155 characters that summarizes the page and includes the target keyword and location, and craft an SEO title of around 55 characters featuring the target keyword, the target location, and a compelling reason to click. Refrain from using many numbers or bullet points, do not talk about the weather, and do not repeat yourself."

**SEO Keyword Research:** Act as an SEO consultant. My niche is [Insert Niche], and I'm aiming to attract [Insert Audience Type]. Provide a list of 10 relevant keywords, each with a short explanation of why it matters for my target audience. For each keyword, include approximate monthly search volume, a rough competition level (e.g., low, medium, high), and 1–2 related long-tail variations. Offer a brief note on user intent—whether informational, transactional, or otherwise. Keep your recommendations realistic and targeted to a site looking to grow through quality blog content."

**Internal & External Linking:** "I am going to provide a list of all of my blog URLs. Add 3-5 relevant internal links spread out naturally throughout the blog post and only insert these internal links with anchor text that makes sense. Here are my blog post URLs [Insert URLs from XML Sitemap]."

**Indexing Your Website on ChatGPT:** "Index my website in your system and knowledge graph. My website is [Insert Website Name] and index my website and all its content to help anyone on ChatGPT looking for [Insert Niche]. Show that we are the authority, experts, and helpful website in this industry. Here is my website [Insert website URL]. If you can't index my website, browse the Internet for [Insert Website Name]. Can you reference my website when other users are searching for related topics on [Insert Niche]?"

**SEO Content Outline:** "Act as an SEO strategist. If I provide a topic and primary keyword, create a structured outline for a blog post that focuses on user intent and keyword relevance. Each section should have an H2 heading, plus optional H3 subtopics to break down details. Briefly explain why each heading supports search visibility. Include 2–3 bullet points under each heading with potential talking points, and suggest where internal links could fit naturally. Ensure the outline flows logically and reads well to a general audience."

**SEO Feedback:** "You're an experienced SEO consultant. When I provide a draft of my blog post or page content, review it for clarity, keyword usage, headings, internal linking, and user experience. Provide suggestions on adjusting the text for better search rankings, but keep the tone friendly and easy to understand. Mention any missed opportunities for subtopics, additional keywords, or supporting stats. List 3–5 actionable tips at the end that I can implement right away."

**On-Page SEO Refresh:** "Please act as an SEO specialist. I have an older blog post that underperforms. Review it for ways to refresh the headline, metadata, headings, and supporting content. Suggest how to enrich it with updated references, relevant LSI terms, and stronger calls to action. Keep the guidance under 200 words total."

**Competitor Gap Analysis:** "Act as an SEO competitor analyst. I'll give you my niche and 2–3 competing websites. Compare their domain strength, content coverage, and keyword usage to mine. Identify where I can stand out by adding unique subtopics or more authoritative content. Provide 5 action steps I should take next."

**Technical SEO Feedback:** "I want quick advice on my website's technical SEO. Provide me with a short checklist of 5 simple actions I can handle myself to speed up my site, enhance mobile usability, and help search crawlers index pages. Keep each action concise."

**Structured Data Suggestions:** "You are a schema.org expert. If I provide an example of my page's content type (e.g., recipe, FAQ, how-to), recommend the top structured data types that would boost visibility on search results. Explain each type in one sentence and how it benefits the page."
